# Sprint 08 — Registry Intelligence

## Goal
Make the registry adaptive to connector health and performance.

## Single-response tasks
1. Add connector scoring and prioritization logic to the registry.
2. Track connector health history and reliability over time.
3. Implement basic self-healing and repair strategies for connectors.
4. Add automatic disable and re-enable rules based on health outcomes.
5. Improve logging and diagnostics for registry decisions.
6. Add configuration for retry policy and concurrency limits.
7. Add tests for registry scoring and recovery behavior.

## Deliverables
- The registry can adapt to connector health and reliability.
- Self-healing and recovery logic are available.

## Definition of Done
- Registry intelligence is active and configurable.
- Connector health and recovery behavior are observable.
- Tests cover the main registry behaviors.
